The New Student Enrollment Experience

The New Student Enrollment experience includes learning about the requirements for your college, meeting with an academic advisor to develop a course schedule, registering for classes, obtaining an NU ID card, and
learning about programs and issues related to success.

The Campaign for Nebraska

The University of Nebraska Foundation is involved in a campaign to raise $1.2 billion to fund university priorities such as student scholarships, faculty recruitment, research and program support.

Stand Up For Nebraska

With a membership in or gift to the Nebraska Alumni Association, alumni, fans and friends have the opportunity to make a tremendous impact on the University of Nebraska.
