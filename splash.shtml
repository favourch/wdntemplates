Schlichting is UNL's 14th Truman Scholar

Emily Schlichting, a junior majoring in communication studies and political science, is the 14th UNL student to earn a Truman Scholarship. The honors student is an advocate for health care reform, and plans to use the $30,000 award to pursue a master's degree in public health and public policy.
